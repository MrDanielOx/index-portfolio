Daniels Best films of all time!

My top 3 films

Avengers: Infinity war

Avengers: Infinity War" because it's just so epic, you know?
I mean, come on, it's got all these amazing characters from the Marvel universe teaming up against Thanos.
And speaking of Thanos, he's not your typical bad guy; he's complex and actually makes you think.
Plus, the movie doesn't just rely on action; it's got some real emotional depth.

Thor: Ragnarok


Thor: Ragnarok" is a fan favorite in the Marvel Cinematic Universe for several reasons.
Firstly, it introduces a refreshing change in tone, blending humor and action in a way that feels both natural and entertaining.
The film's vibrant visual style, coupled with director Taika Waititi's unique comedic sensibilities, breathes new life into the character of Thor,
making him more relatable and likable than ever before.
Additionally, the dynamic chemistry between the characters, especially Thor and Hulk, adds depth and charm to the story. Overall,
"Thor: Ragnarok" is celebrated for its humor, visual spectacle, and compelling character development, making it a standout entry in the MCU.

Bohemian Rhapsody

Bohemian Rhapsody" for its captivating portrayal of Freddie Mercury's journey, electrifying musical performances,
and Rami Malek's transformative portrayal of the iconic frontman. It's a celebration of Queen's music and the enduring legacy of one of rock's greatest bands,
making it a film I truly cherish.
